General Information

  • Infinity Program is a loyalty program run by Lemon Tree Hotels that allows customers to enroll and enjoy exclusive benefits, privileges, and rewards for their stays.

  • A customer may join the program in any of the following ways:

    1. During their stay at a participating hotel

    2. By registering online at the Brand website: www.lemontreehotels.com

    3. 3. Via the Lemon Tree Hotels Central Reservation Center

  • The Customer must provide the necessary and mandatory details requested when joining – full Legal name (First and last name), Unique and a valid Email ID, and Unique and a valid Mobile number. Member will have to present the Proof of identification on request.

  • The new Member shall accept the Program’s General Terms and Conditions. Once accepted, the Member will benefit fully from all Program services, including access to the different functions of the website (such as accessing Rewards, checking his/her customer account, redeeming Reward Coins, etc).

  • The minimum age to become an Infinity member is 18 years of age or more.

  • No, joining the Infinity Program is free. There are no membership fees, so you can enroll and start earning rewards.

  • You start enjoying the benefits of the Infinity Program immediately upon enrollment. Once you have registered, you can begin earning on eligible stay and avail the member benefits depending upon the tier base.

  • You can view your account details on the brand website, www.lemontreehotels.com, once you log in to your account.

  • Using their personal account, members can view -

    • • Total Coins Balance

    • • Current tier status and its benefits

    • • Coins required for the next tier

    • • Stay history and coins earned on those stays

    • • Redemption request against available coins

    • • Registered personal information with edit permission

  • To add or update your contact details, email address, birthdate, anniversary and other personal information, please login to your Infinity account on our official website and navigate to the profile settings section. You can make all necessary changes directly online or you may send/write the request to Infinity customer care at Infinityrewards@lemontreehotels.com to get the details updated.

  • Yes, members can check his or her reservation history (past/completed bookings) on the brand website once they logged-in to his or her account- under the “My Trip” section. Please note that the reservations will be reflected in your account 24-48 hours post checkout. To add missing reservations please contact Infinity Customer Centre (CRC) at Infinityrewards@lemontreehotels.com.

    The future reservation of the member is not available/ cannot to be seen on the brand website for now. The functionality is under progress and will be available to members soon.

    2. Cancellation/amendment done 2 days before arrival: 25% Refund of Infinity Coins

    3. Cancellation/amendment done 1 day before arrival: No Refund

All eligible charges incurred during a qualifying stay will contribute to earning coins.

  • Infinity Coins cannot be transferred to another account, even with consent. Coins are non-transferable and are intended solely for the account holder's use.

Earning and Redeeming Coins

  • Infinity Coins can be earned on eligible spending at participating hotels.

  • No, Infinity Coins are only earned on direct bookings made through our brand website www.lemontreehotels.com, Central Reservation Center at 9911701701, or walk-in at participating hotels.

  • You can redeem your Infinity Coins for complimentary nights at participating properties. Please visit www.lemontreehotels.com/member/redeem to explore your “Redeem” options, or email us at Infinityrewards@lemontreehotels.com to initiate a redemption request.

  • No, Infinity Coins cannot be converted into cash. Members can redeem these coins for room nights only at participating hotels.

  • Yes, blackout dates may apply during peak seasons and special events.

  • Yes, there are blackout dates when you cannot use your coins for stays. These dates are set by Lemon Tree Hotels and may limited room availability. To check availability, please contact us at Infinityrewards@lemontreehotels.com

  • Infinity Coins cannot be used for booking conference or banquet rooms.

  • When booking through the official website, members can earn Infinity Coins based on current tier. For detailed earning rates, please refer to the terms and conditions.

  • When booking through the call center, or corporate rate, you will earn coins according to the rate and booking channel. The earning rate for coins may vary based on the type of tier level and booking channel. For detailed earning rates, please refer to the terms and conditions.

  • A “Ineligible" is defined as; (1) Stays booked through a reseller, tour operator, traditional/local travel agents and third-party online travel agency (such as Expedia.com, booking.com, make my trip or any other OTAs) are not considered as Eligible Stays and do not earn Coin or counted to be Eligible nights for a tier upgrade. (2) Room rates for airline staff and crew, airline layovers, airline transient, charter,(3) employee rates, (4) complimentary rooms, (5) special negotiated guaranteed company packages, (6) group booking rates, (7) banquets, conferences, weddings, events or organized tours.

  • Please note that the maximum rewards for booked rooms are as follows:

    1. The maximum reward for rooms booked by a person for a single day is 3 rooms.

    2. For example, if a member books 10 rooms for check-in on August 15 and check-out on August 16, points will only be awarded for three rooms. No rewards will be given for the remaining seven rooms.

    3. The maximum reward for rooms booked by a person for multiple dates is 7 room nights.

      For example, if a member books a room with check-in on August 15 and check-out on August 25, points will be awarded for only seven room nights. No rewards will be given for the remaining three room nights.

  • No, you cannot use your coins to pay for food and beverage (F&B) services, spa treatments, transportation, or similar services.

  • No, it is not possible to use a combination of coins and cash for room nights, F&B, spa services, or transportation. Coins can only be used against room stays.

  • Members can redeem a maximum of 7 room nights/3 rooms per request. To submit a redemption request, please visit www.lemontreehotels.com/member/redeem to explore “Redeem” options, or email us at Infinityrewards@lemontreehotels.com to initiate a redemption request.

  • No, additional discounts cannot be combined with point redemptions. When redeeming Infinity Coins, the coins will be applied to the room amount only and cannot be combined with special offers and packages. However, member can avail the current tier benefits during the stay.
